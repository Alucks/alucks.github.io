#mytinyatlasLONDON

When
July 21
6-9 PM
Until SEP 3

Where
Dulwich Picture Gallery
Gallery Rd
Southwark SE21 7AD
UK

To celebrate the Kickstarter launch of our new camera bag, Solas, Tiny Atlas Quarterly curated a print photography show at Alite Design’s Outpost in the Mission, SF.

Light, the defintion of Solas in Gaelic, was the show’s theme and more than 9,OOO images were submitted from TAQ’s Instragram community with the #mytinyatlasSOLAS. Guest curated by Creative Director, Cory Jacobs, and TAQ’s own Emily Nathan, the following selects were printed for the show, in partnership with printing partner Social Print Studios.
