#mytinyatlasLONDON

When
July 19th - 22nd

Where
Shoreditch, Croydon,
Dulwich, Greenwich
London - United Kingdom

London; one of the most loved cities in the world, and through the eyes of the Tiny Atlas Quarterly community we brought #mytinyatlasLondon to life.

With thanks to our friends: Fujfilm Instax North America, WOW air, Paper Chase Press, Ohana Communications, The Producer, Visit London, Visit Greenwich, Croydon Council, Rise Gallery, Journy, Sean Spellman, , ANdAZ London, and
the Dulwich Picture Gallery.

Three community led photo walks, one real-time picture gallery and over 9,000 submissions to date, this was your #mytinyatlasLondon
