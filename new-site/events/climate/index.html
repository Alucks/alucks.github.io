#mytinyatlasCLIMATE

When
Dec 12th - 14th, 2017

Where
302 Folsom Street,
San Francisco, CA
London - United Kingdom

Climate change has altered our landscapes and poses dangerous threats to our environment. Our Tiny Atlas community has captured photos and stories in showing what #mytinyatlasCLIMATE means to them. Through proceeds from a benefit show and silent auction, TAQ and our partner, The East Cut, are directly assisting non-profit organizations that fight and respond to the effects of climate change. This is #mytinyatlasCLIMATE.
