Tiny Atlas Quarterly x Alohilani Giveaway

Tiny Atlas recently visited Oahu and now you can experience Waikiki just how we did. On Thursday December 21st enter to win a 2 night stay at the Alohilani Resort on our Tiny Atlas Quarterly Instagram.

What you Win:

A 2-night stay in a Premier Ocean-Front Room at Alohilani with complimentary daily breakfast for 2 at the on-site restaurant, Lychee.

The premier ocean front rooms, located in the Beachside tower features stunning views of the Pacific Ocean. Fashioned with a bright and modern beachside cottage vibe, each room boasts unobstructed Waikiki Beach views perfect for watching the surf (and surfers) or enjoying a colorful sunset.

All guestrooms include an outdoor lanai (balcony).

How to Enter:

1. Tag a friend you want to escape to Oahu with on the Tiny Atlas Quarterly Instagram post featuring the giveaway on December 21st.
2. Follow the Alohilani Resort Instagram

Winner Notification:

The Tiny Atlas + Alohilani Giveaway will be live on Instagram for 24 hours, from 10am PST December 21st until 10am PST December 22nd. On December 22nd one winner will be chosen at random and notified.

Black-out days: December 24th, 25th and 31st, January 1st and July 4.

Tiny Altas will also post the winner on the giveaway post on Instagram.

Good Luck and Mahalo!
