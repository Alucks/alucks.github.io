Creative Retreat:
Lummi Island

When
July 19th - 23rd, 2018

This little island is considered one of the best kept secrets of the San Juan archipelago, surrounded by neighboring beaches, rural mountains, state parks, and a quirky art scene. Stay on a 5 acre property with glamping and cottage accommodations bordering a large edible garden. Enhance your palate with fresh caught seafood, local veggies, and zesty meals throughout the trip. On Lummi Island, we will never be short of entertainment as we hike, boat, whale watch, and embark on a sea foraging kayak trip all in a weekend's time.

Tiny Atlas Quarterly Mendocino

When
May 17, 2018

Join Tiny Atlas’ creative community for a weekend retreat in Mendocino, California. Stay at modern, fully-furnished tents in the middle of a forest of coastal pines and Douglas firs overlooking the Pacific. All meals will be prepared with heart and creativity by the Catering Cousins, including some freshly foraged ingredients and California grown produce. Surrounded by lush canyons and crisp ocean air, we invite you to delve into your photography and writing skills and create alongside others in the Tiny Atlas community.
